BIG BROTHER
Rules
The show features 16 contestants who live together in a specially constructed house that is isolated from the outside world.
The name is inspired by Big Brother from George Orwell's novel Nineteen Eighty-Four, and the housemates are continuously monitored for 3 months during their stay in the house by live television cameras as well as personal audio microphones.
